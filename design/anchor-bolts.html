Applications of anchor bolts.

Trusted across industries where the foundation matters — structural steel, industrial equipment, critical infrastructure, and utility-scale renewables.

01 — Construction
Structural steel columns, beams & framing.
Base plate anchorage for moment frames, braced frames, and gravity columns.
02 — Industrial machinery
Heavy equipment & machine bases.
Holding-down bolts for presses, mills, compressors, and production lines.
03 — Infrastructure
Bridges, highways, tunnels.
Long-service-life HDG and stainless anchors for seismic and wind loading.
04 — Renewable energy
Wind turbines & solar foundations.
Large-diameter anchor assemblies engineered for utility-scale generation.
